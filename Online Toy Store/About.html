Monopoly Game of Thrones Board Game

Monopoly gameplay gets reimagined for fans of HBO's Game of Thrones. In this edition of the Monopoly game, the gameboard, packaging, tokens, money, Chance cards, and game pieces are based on the show. And, one player becomes the Master of Coin and is in charge of The Royal Treasury's money instead of having a bank and banker like in classic Monopoly. The game comes with cardboard coins inspired by the currency in the Game of Thrones TV show in place of Monopoly money. It also features an Iron Throne card holder that plays the Game of Thrones theme song.
